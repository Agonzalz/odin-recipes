Enchiladas

Description

Chicken rolled inside of a tortilla that is cooked in oil and dipped in red chile sauce. Topped with melted mozarella cheese/mexican cheese, onion, lettuce, and optional sour cream.

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts shredded
  • garlic cloves
  • ground cumin
  • monterey jack cheese
  • Mexican Queso Fresco
  • corn tortillas
  • salt
  • onion
  • pepper
  • oil
  • dried ancho chile
  • dried guajillo chile
  • oregano

Steps

  1. Cook chicken in a large pot over medium heat with water. Cook for about 25 minutes.
  2. Reserve some of the water the chicken was cooked in, about 2 cups of broth.
  3. Shred chicken with two forks
  4. If using the onion (highly recommend) add 2-3 tablespoons of vegetable oil to a skillet over medium high heat and saute for 2 minutes. Stir onions and now add the minced garlic to pan. Cook garlic for about 30 seconds over medium heat.
  5. Add shredded chicken and broth of chicken.
  6. Sprinkle cumin, chili powder, salt and pepper over chicken and stir mixture together. Cook for about 10 to 15 minutes or until chicken broth reduces a bit. You want to make sure chicken stays moist. Set aside.
